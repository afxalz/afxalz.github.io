Afzal Ahmad

I am Research Fellow at the Multi-Robot Systems lab in Czech Technical University in Prague, Czech Republic. I have been working on multi-robot coordination for high speed flights in cluttered environments. My work in supervised of Dr. Martin Saska.

I graduated with a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence from the Czech Technical University in Prague in 2022. During my masters I have worked on decentralized control of multi-UAV systems under the supervision of Dr. Martin Saska. My masters thesis focused on investigating multi-UAV trajectory planning with limited to no information sharing among the UAVs.

Prior to this, I obtained a Bachelor’s in Electronics and Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati in 2019. As an undergraduate researcher, I worked on simulating a swarm of ground robots at the MIST lab, Polytechnique Montreal, Canada.
